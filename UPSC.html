Our Core Programs

A result-oriented approach to mastering the UPSC Civil Services Examination.

Preliminary Exam

Foundation for Success

  • Comprehensive GS Paper-I: History, Geography, Polity, Economy, Environment, and Science & Tech.
  • CSAT Training: Logical reasoning, quantitative aptitude, and comprehension.
  • Practice Ecosystem: Regular topic-wise tests and full-length mock exams.
  • Fundamentals: Concept-oriented teaching with focus on exam pattern clarity.

Mains Exam

Conceptual Depth & Writing

  • GS I–IV Mastery: Analytical coverage with issue-based learning and interlinking.
  • Essay Paper: Structure, themes, and scoring strategies.
  • Optional Papers I & II Intern and indepth focused guidance.
  • Daily Answer Writing: Answer writing practice with personalized feedback.
  • Value Addition: Case studies, flowcharts, and current affairs integration.
  • Medium Support: Tamil Medium guidance available if needed.

Interview Guidance

Personality Development

  • Mock Interviews: Realistic simulations with expert panel.
  • DAF Analysis: One-on-one mentorship based on your application.
  • Soft Skills: Training on body language, communication, and confidence.
  • Current Affairs: Briefings on trending national and international topics.
